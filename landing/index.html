How It Works

Install once. Every AI knows you.

Three components that work silently in the background.

01

Install & forget

A Chrome extension silently tracks what you browse, build, search, and read. Runs entirely on your machine. Nothing leaves your device.

02

Persona builds overnight

Every night, PersonaLayer analyzes your behavior and builds a structured profile: what you're building, how you think, what obsesses you, how you communicate.

03

Every AI knows you instantly

Via MCP server, Claude, Cursor, Perplexity, and any AI agent automatically gets your full context before you type a word. No configuration needed.
